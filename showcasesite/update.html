The AMR Challenge

Misuse of antibiotics in livestock leads to unsafe milk and meat, causing hidden health crises and export losses for India.

Antimicrobial resistance threatens public health, farmer livelihoods, and the global reputation of Indian food products.

Agrisense

India’s Smart Antimicrobial Tracking & Compliance Platform

This isn't another government portal. It isn't another complicated app. We threw all that out. Agrisense is an ecosystem of trust. It's the simplest, most powerful way to ensure the food on our tables is safe.

Let me show you how it works. This is the magic.

We started with the person this whole system depends on: the farmer. Most people would try to force them to use a complicated app. We didn't. We asked, "What's the one tool every single farmer has?" It's a simple phone.

So, when a farmer treats an animal, they just make a phone call. They speak in their own language. That's it. No forms, no typing. It just works. Our system listens, understands, and logs everything, instantly. It’s magical.

For veterinarians, we created a tool that feels less like software and more like an intelligent assistant. All the animal's history, all the data they need, right in their hand. It helps them make the best decision, every single time. Simple. Powerful.

And for the government? We give them something they've never had before: clarity. We take all that chaos and turn it into a beautiful, simple dashboard. A God's-eye view of the entire country. They can see problems before they happen.

QR Traceability
